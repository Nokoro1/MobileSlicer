About

Built for Android, not squeezed onto it.

MobileSlicer keeps the normal 3D printing steps, but makes them usable on a phone or tablet.

Made for phones and tablets.

MobileSlicer lets you open models from Android, work with touch-friendly controls, and keep the print flow on your phone or tablet.

Open a model, choose your printer and material, slice on the device, check the G-code, then export, share, or print directly to supported network printers.

Source code is available on GitHub.

In the app

MobileSlicer covers the full print path.

The app covers the pieces that matter first: import, plate setup, profiles, local slicing, preview, export, and saved plates.

Import

STL and 3MF

Open STL and 3MF files from storage or a share sheet, then prepare them in the workspace.

Workspace

Plate setup

Move, rotate, scale, duplicate, arrange, and save models from the phone or tablet workspace.

Profiles

Printer, filament, process

Pick the active printer, material, and process profile before slicing, with custom profile work built into the app.

Slicing

Local slicing

Run the slice on the Android device. No cloud slicer or desktop handoff is required.

Preview

G-code you can inspect

Review sliced layers, line types, time, filament use, and layer ranges before exporting the print file.

Calibration

Printer tuning prints

Create calibration jobs for pressure advance, flow, temperature, retraction, VFA, input shaping, and more.
