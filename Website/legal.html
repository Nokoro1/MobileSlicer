MobileSlicer

MobileSlicer is a local, touch-first 3D printing slicer for Android. It supports STL import, supported 3MF mesh/project loading, plate preparation, profile selection, local slicing, G-code preview, export, sharing, and supported network printing on phones and tablets.

The slicing engine used by MobileSlicer is derived from OrcaSlicer and related open-source slicer projects. OrcaSlicer profile concepts, slicer terminology, and engine behavior are credited to the OrcaSlicer project and its upstream authors.

Privacy

MobileSlicer is designed around local slicing on your Android device. The app does not require a MobileSlicer account or automatic cloud slicing. Optional Thingiverse sign-in uses Thingiverse's authorization flow so users can search and import files they select through the official API. Model files and G-code are not uploaded to MobileSlicer servers.

Files and printer data can be sent when you choose to export, share, connect to a printer, or use a configured print service. The full privacy details are on the Privacy page.
